Success Storie

I have scored 91% in the SSC Board Exam which was held in March 2013. It was indeed a moment of rejoicing. I always dreamt of pursuing my higher studies in the field of Science. Thereby, after the results were declared, I have applied for Science as my stream for Junior College and secured my admission to my dream college. One of the biggest threats that stood in front of me was the fees of the college because my parent lacked a strong financial background. Therefore, to ease my studies, they were continuously trying to approach every person they knew so that the college finances would be managed by receiving the scholarship. One of my neighbour told Ammi that there is a person who looks after the scholarship and it was none other than Mr. Sharique Raza Shaikh of Rise India Foundation. He have explained to me and my parents that there is an option of availing scholarship from the Government which will cover my fees. It was a moment of relief. He was the one who has kept our hopes high and has extended all support for securing a scholarship. He has helped me right from filling the application form to uploading the necessary documents. Months have passed after filling the application and suddenly one fine day I received a message on the registered mobile number with the bank, mentioning that your account is credited with scholarship amount. The message was a turning point in our lives and proved that if you can have faith and patience, things will surely turn down into your favour. This was possible because of my parent who had faith in Mr. Sharique Raza Shaikh that he will possibly give a way out to manage the fees. I would like to thanks him and Rise India Foundation for not only helping me during that situation but also helped me in pursuing a career that I would always love to do.

I am Shaikh Gulam Moin, a resident of Mumbai. Currently, I am in First Year of Masters in Pharmacy (Pharmaceutical Quality Assurance) + MBA (Pharmaceutical Technology and Health Care Management) from SVKM’s NMIMS which is an integrated three years course
